AMD + IFVG model

Accumulation → Manipulation → IFVG → Distribution

Range first, trap move second, imbalance flip third, expansion last.

A
Accumulation Price moves sideways and builds orders. Let the range form before acting.
M
Manipulation One side of the range gets swept to trap breakout traders.
I
IFVG confirmation The imbalance flips and confirms the market likely wants the other side.
D
Distribution Price expands away from the range. Manage your trade into clean targets.

Strategy 01

AMD + IFVG Reversal

Let price build a range first. Wait for manipulation to sweep one side. Then look for the IFVG or imbalance flip that confirms the trap failed. The better entry is on the retest, not the first move.

Strategy 02

Sweep + BOS Pullback

Price sweeps liquidity first, then breaks structure in the new direction. Do not enter on the sweep candle. Wait for the break, then wait again for the pullback into a good entry area.

Strategy 03

Liquidity Sweep + Breaker Retest

After a sweep and strong structure break, the last failed candle or mini zone can become a breaker. If price comes back and respects that area, it can be a continuation entry.
